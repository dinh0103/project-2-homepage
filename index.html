Mission Statement

BURNSVILLE FESTIVAL AND FIRE MUSTER IS A 501C3 NON-PROFIT ORGANIZATION COMMITTED TO PROMOTING BURNSVILLE’S CULTURALLY DIVERSE COMMUNITY BY HIGHLIGHTING THE QUALITY OF LIFE IN OUR COMMUNITY THROUGH A VARIETY OF INCLUSIVE CULTURAL EVENTS AND FUN-FILLED EXPERIENCES.

We are always looking for more citizens to get involved. Please contact us for more information!

PARADE MAP

(Red Line) Grab a spot to see the parade on both sides 130th Street and the northbound lane of Nicollet Avenue and Civic Center Parkway.

(Blue Line) Positions 1-37 will line up on 130th Street straight east of the parade route shown in red.

(Green Line) Positions 38 & Higher will line up on Parkwood North of 130th, facing 130th towards the parade route.

(Gold Line) Community Fire Trucks will line up on Parkwood South of 130th on the West/Left side of the street facing 130th. The MNFEC vintage trucks will be on the East side of the street.
